Pillar 1 · Intelligence Data Fabric
The Intelligence Data Fabric

Your System of Record
for All Security Data

The most capable AI is only as good as the data it works with. In 2026 we're completing the foundation: a Security Data Lakehouse that ingests any source, normalises it automatically, and makes it available for detection, investigation, and compliance reporting.

Universal Ingestion - Any Source
OS, network, cloud, application, and third-party security tools - all flowing through a single lightweight collector. Automatic classification and normalisation to the Open Cybersecurity Schema Framework (OCSF).
Detect Threats on Your Logs
Run the full Detection Fabric against every log you ingest - not just endpoint and network. Threats hiding in firewall logs, cloud audit trails, and application events surface automatically.
Historical Investigation On Demand
Query across your full archive instantly when an incident occurs. No more limitations on how far back you can look - just ask the question.
DORA & NIS2 Compliance Module Q3 2026
Pre-packaged evidence outputs for DORA and NIS2 obligations. Compliance reporting that writes itself from the data already in your fabric.

Security Clarity
For Every Stakeholder
